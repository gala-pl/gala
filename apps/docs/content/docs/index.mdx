Gala is a **classical language whose compiler enforces the laws of quantum mechanics** —
no-cloning, reversibility, and safe uncomputation — while making hybrid quantum-classical
machine learning ergonomic end to end.

## Why Gala?

Quantum programs are never purely quantum. They are classical programs that orchestrate quantum
kernels, read measurements, and feed parameters back in a loop. Gala is designed from the ground
up for this hybrid reality.

- **Correctness you can't opt out of** — physically meaningless programs are unrepresentable
- **Explicit classical/quantum boundary** — measurement is visible in the types and never silent
- **Differentiability as a language feature** — `grad` is a native operator
- **Great DX from day one** — LSP, simulator, formatter, and test runner ship with the language
- **Backend-polymorphic** — the same source runs on simulators or real hardware
